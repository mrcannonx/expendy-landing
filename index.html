Features

Everything to master your spending

No bank connections. No complex setup. Just speak and see where your money goes.

Voice-First Logging

Just speak naturally. "Lunch twelve dollars" or "Uber to work twenty-five." No typing, no forms, no friction.

AI Smart Categories

Expendy auto-sorts spending into 12 categories: food, transport, shopping, entertainment, and more. It learns your habits.

Visual Insights

Donut charts and breakdowns show exactly where your money goes. Weekly and monthly trends at a glance.
