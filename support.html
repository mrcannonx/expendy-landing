Frequently Asked Questions

How does voice tracking work?

Tap the microphone button and speak naturally. Say something like "coffee five dollars" or "groceries forty-two fifty." Our AI understands natural speech and automatically categorizes your expense.

Is my data private?

Yes. All your expense data is stored locally on your device. Voice recognition is processed on-device. We never see or store your financial information.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases." Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Export Data to download all your expenses as a CSV file that you can open in any spreadsheet app.

How do recurring expenses work?

Go to Settings > Recurring Expenses to set up bills and subscriptions. Choose the frequency (daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, yearly) and Expendy will auto-log them for you.
